Frequently Asked Questions

How does FSBOOS make money?

We charge agents a platform fee (monthly subscription + small per-transaction fee). Sellers use the platform for free. Agents set their own rates and keep the majority of their commissions.

Are FSBOOS agents licensed?

Yes, all agents on FSBOOS are fully licensed real estate professionals. We verify licenses and require professional liability insurance.

What's the catch with discount agents?

No catch. These agents use FSBOOS technology to reduce overhead, allowing them to charge less while earning the same or more through higher volume. You get the same services for less.

Can I negotiate with agents on FSBOOS?

Absolutely. Agents post their standard rates, but you can request custom proposals. Many sellers negotiate hybrid models or bundled services.

How do I become a FSBOOS agent?

Apply at fsboos.com/agents. We review your license, experience, and business model. Approved agents get full access to our white-label platform and seller network.

Is FSBOOS available outside ?

Currently we serve only (Phoenix, Tucson, and all metro areas). Expansion to other markets coming in 2027.
