Agent FAQ

Do I need to be a broker to use FSBOOS?

No. Licensed agents can join under our broker-of-record. If you have your own brokerage license, we have special broker plans with additional features.

What's the minimum commitment?

No long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We're confident you'll stay because the platform works.

How do I get paid?

Commissions go to our broker-of-record, then we pay you within 48 hours of closing minus your 10% platform fee. Simple and fast.

Can I set my own commission rates?

Absolutely! You set your rates. Most agents on FSBOOS charge 1-2% or flat fees. You can also offer tiered packages.

What about E&O insurance?

You're covered under our group E&O policy. No separate insurance needed.

How many leads will I get?

Lead volume varies by market and your activity. Active agents typically receive 5-15 qualified leads per month from the FSBOOS network, plus leads from your own marketing.
