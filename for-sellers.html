Seller FAQ

Is this really free for sellers?

Yes! It costs nothing to list your home and receive agent offers. You only pay if you choose to work with an agent, and then you pay their commission at closing.

Are these discount agents any good?

Absolutely. These are experienced, licensed agents who use FSBOOS technology to reduce overhead. Many are former top producers at big brokerages. Check their reviews and sales history.

What's the difference between 1% and 6% agents?

The commission rate. Most services are identical: MLS listing, photos, showings, negotiation, contract handling. The discount comes from technology and volume, not service cuts.

Can I negotiate with agents?

Yes! Agents post their standard rates, but you can request custom proposals. Many sellers negotiate hybrid models or additional services.
